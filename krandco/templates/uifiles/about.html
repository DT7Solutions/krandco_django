Cultivation and Sourcing

K.R & Co collaborates with tobacco farmers to cultivate high-quality tobacco plants.

Harvesting

Farmers hand-harvest mature tobacco leaves, selecting based on size, color, and texture for quality.

Curing and Drying

Leaves undergo curing (air, flue, sun) to remove moisture, enhancing flavor and aroma.

Grading and Sorting

Leaves graded by color, texture, size, and condition to ensure consistency.

Quality Assurance

K.R & Co ensures consistent quality from cultivation to distribution, emphasizing excellence.

Global Distribution

K.R & Co globally distributes tobacco, ensuring timely delivery and customer satisfaction through efficient logistics.

About K.R & Co

K.R & Co traces its origins back to KRK, founded by Mr. Karpurapu Ramakrishna Murthy in 1948, with a primary focus on international tobacco trading. The company underwent a name change to K. R & Co in 1998 under the leadership of Managing Partner Mr. K. Santhi Bhushan.

K.R & Co maintains stringent quality control measures throughout the sourcing and production process, working closely with tobacco farmers and adhering to international standards and regulations.

K.R & Co serves clients across European countries, Russia, the USA, Egypt, and Africa, leveraging its extensive global network and strategic partnerships.
